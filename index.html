How to play

Connect the two words by changing one letter at a time. Every step must be a real word.

COLD→ CORD→ WORD→ WARD→ WARM

Each step changes exactly one letter. Try to reach the target in as few steps as possible!

Choose your difficulty:

Easy — 3 letters Medium — 4 letters Hard — 5 letters

A new daily puzzle every day. Keep your streak alive by playing every day!

Stuck? Use the Hint button — each hint adds 1 step to your score.

🎓 For Teachers — Primary & Secondary

Word Chain works across Key Stages. The three difficulty levels map naturally to different ages and abilities — pick the level that suits your class, or let students choose their own. Competitive, focused, and (quietly) doing real literacy work.

✓Free, no login or app download required — just share a link
✓Works on any device — school iPads, Chromebooks, laptops and phones
✓Three difficulty levels suit mixed abilities across year groups
✓Practice mode gives unlimited puzzles beyond the daily one — ideal for longer sessions
✓After completing, players see the optimal shortest solution — great for class discussion
✓Live leaderboards with school names — kids compete with classmates and other schools
✓Daily streak encourages students to return each day

Primary — KS2 (Years 3–6) — Easy (3-letter) and Medium (4-letter) puzzles support vocabulary, spelling patterns and word recognition. Ideal as a starter, early-finisher task, or homework. Pairs with the English National Curriculum's word-reading and spelling strands.

Secondary — KS3 & KS4 (Years 7–11) — Hard (5-letter) puzzles extend vocabulary range, develop orthographic fluency, and reinforce morphological awareness. Useful as a warm-up for English lessons, a retrieval activity, or GCSE-level revision for etymology and word-building. Form tutors can run the daily puzzle in registration as a whole-class challenge.

SEN & intervention groups — The single-letter-change structure makes phonological differences visible, which supports dyslexic and SpLD learners. Practice mode lets you run as many puzzles as needed at a consistent difficulty.

🔗 Create a custom puzzle link
Enter any two words and share a direct link — students open it and play your custom word chain. Great for vocabulary lists.
🌍 For ESL & EFL Teachers

Word Chain is a powerful vocabulary tool for English language learners. Changing one letter at a time makes students focus on how spelling and meaning shift — a natural, game-based approach to morphological awareness.

✓Builds spelling pattern recognition — students see exactly how one letter change alters a word
✓Vocabulary expansion through word families — CAT → BAT → BIT → BIG reveals connected clusters
✓Low-pressure, high-engagement — students focus on the puzzle, not on fear of making mistakes
✓Works for A1–B2 levels — Easy (3-letter) words suit beginners; Medium and Hard suit intermediate learners
✓Use the custom link generator above to build puzzles from your own vocabulary lists
✓No login, no app — works instantly on any device in any country

TESOL / TEFL use — Suitable as a warmer, cooler, or independent activity. Supports phonics awareness, orthographic knowledge, and productive vocabulary. Can be used with word lists from any CEFR-aligned course.
