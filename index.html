How to play

Connect the two words by changing one letter at a time. Every step must be a real word.

COLD→ CORD→ WORD→ WARD→ WARM

Each step changes exactly one letter. Try to reach the target in as few steps as possible!

Choose your difficulty:

Easy — 3 letters Medium — 4 letters Hard — 5 letters

A new daily puzzle every day. Keep your streak alive by playing every day!

Stuck? Use the Hint button — each hint adds 1 step to your score.
